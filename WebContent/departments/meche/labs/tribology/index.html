TRIBOLOGY AND OIL TESTING LAB

The mechanical engineering department provides expertise that includes laboratory testing and data interpretation capabilities. The testing facilities include tribological test for friction lubrication, wear, surface analysis .samples tested include lubricants , metals ceramics and many other materials.

Equipment Details
Tribometer Reciprocating type
Tribotester Reciprocating /rotary/scratch testing with nanoindentor.
Oil analyser Viscosity/conductivity/particle count with highresolution. microscope and camera embedded with the system
Ultrasonic cleaner
Ceramic cutting machine
Oven

Tribology deals with interacting surfaces in relative motion, friction, wear, and lubrication.The field of tribology is extremely diverse with challenges in many different areas of study including: materials science, fluid dynamics, contact mechanics, dynamics, heat transfer, chemistry, physics, controls, surface characterization, and many more.

The Tribology Lab in Mechanical Engineeering Department at NIT Srinagar is actively involved in a wide variety of Tribological Research Projects and is equipped with many facilities for students and Research Scholars.
